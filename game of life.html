The rules of Game of life

1. If a box has life and it has less than 2 neighbors. It dies of loneliness. The box becomes lifeless next generation.

2. If a box has life and it has more than 3 neighbors. It dies of overpopulation. The box becomes lifeless next generation.

3. If a box has life and it has 2 to 3 neighbors. Nothing changes for this box.

4. If a box has no life and it has 3 neighbors, the box in next generation fills with life because of reproduction.

5. You may change the above rules for difference result.
